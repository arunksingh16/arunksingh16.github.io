LLM Council Beta

PYTHON / AGENTS / BEDROCK

Instead of asking a single LLM (like ChatGPT or Claude) for an answer, LLM Council Beta assembles a council of multiple AI models that independently answer, peer-review, and synthesize a thoroughly vetted response that leverages collective intelligence.

  • Stage 1 (Generation): Multiple models independently answer your question to bring diverse perspectives.
  • Stage 2 (Peer-Review): Models anonymously peer-review each other's answers to cross-examine and identify gaps.
  • Stage 3 (Synthesis): A Chairman model synthesizes the reviewed responses into a balanced, vetted final answer.
